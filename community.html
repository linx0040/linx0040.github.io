Community Guide

Guilds

A guild is essentially an extended buddy list with some extra benefits.
Any given character can be a member of only one guild.
However, unless you are the guild leader, you may leave your guild at any time, leaving you free to find another.

Some benefits of being in a guild include:

  • Receiving special HP/MP potions and W.ATK/M.ATK buff potions every week
  • Receiving increased amount of mesos from monster drops by a set amount
  • Experiencing decreased EXP loss due to death, multiplicatively
  • The ability to purchase items from NPCs at a cheaper price
  • Experiencing increased success rate of normal scrolls and spell traces
  • A chance that your item will not be destroyed by a scroll
  • A chance that the upgrade slot of an item will not be used up if the scroll failed
  • Increases in damage towards boss monsters for a limited time
  • Increases in DEF ignored for a limited time
  • Increases in total damage output for a limited time

Discord

Discord is an all-in-one voice and text chat for gamers that's free, secure, and works on both your desktop and phone.
Users can download the desktop app for better in-game support or use the web version in a web browser.

There are various types of Discord servers players can join.
All players are encouraged to join the Official MapleStory Discord and the /r/Maplestory Subreddit Discord.
Players from Bera can join the the Bera Trading Discord to interact with other players to sell/buy in-game items.
Players who have joined a guild can also join their guild discords if there exists one.

Reddit

Reddit is a social news aggregation, web content rating, and discussion website.
Registered members submit content to the site such as links, text posts, and images, which are then voted up or down by other members.
Posts are organized by subject into boards called "subreddits".
There is a Maplestory subreddit: /r/Maplestory.

The Maplestory subreddit is a good place for players to gather and discuss aspects of the game.
The site is well-organized and well-moderated.
The subreddit also provides additional guides and detailed information for new and existing players.
