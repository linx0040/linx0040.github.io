Cash Guide

Nexon Cash (NX) is virtual currency used in all games serviced by Nexon America, Inc.
NX can be spent to purchase in-game items, accessories, and services.
In Canada, a $10 Karma Koin card will yield 7,800 NX.

NX is most commonly used in Maplestory to purchase items in the Cash Shop.
The game releases weekly cash shop updates.
Items are made available for purchase for a limited time before they are taken off rotation.
The most commonly purchased items are the Royal Hair/Face coupons and the Premium Surprise Style Boxes.

For example, for the September 14th, 2016 rotation:
The Royal hairstyles that players could get randomly were:
Bandana Hair, Boomy Hair, Overgrown Hair, Sun Bleached, Beau Wild Hunter Hair, and Ultra-Bubble Hair for male characters,

Bandana Hair, Chic Kitten Hair, Kitty Hair, Lively Waved, Muse Hair, and Soprano Hair for female characters.

The Royal facestyles that players could get randomly were:
Nue Face, Bright Eyes, Marble Eyes, Piercing Gaze, Arrogant Face, and Puppy Eyes Face for male characters,

Takeno Konoko Face, Teen Drama Face, Bright Eyes, Marble Eyes, Piercing Gaze, and Wounded Look for female characters.

Royal Hairstyle Coupon and Royal Facestyle coupons cost 3,300 NX each.

The Premium Surprise Style Boxes contained items such as:
Umbral Coat, Umbral Earrings, Umbral Boots, Magic Tome Weapon, Umbral Cap, Umbral Attire, Umbral Shoes, Umbral Cloak, Red Cloud Top, Red Cloud Bottom (F), Red Cloud Shoes, Red Cloud Cape, Red Cloud Bottom (M), Red Cloud Chat Ring, and Red Cloud Label Ring.

An additional seasonal style box was available this rotation.
The Autumn Surprise Style Box contained items such as:
Ribbon Boy School Look (M), Hot Pink Backpack, Blue Notebook Quote Ring, Tania Cloak, Tania Gloves, Tania Bolero (F), Tania Tartan Skirt (F), Tania En Fuego, White Bread, 80's Knit Pullover, Blue Pencil Label Ring, Vintage Khaki Cap, Preppy Knit Vest, Mercury Washed Jeans (M), and Scoreboard Label Ring.

Surprise Style Boxes cost 3,400 nx for 1 or 34,000 for a pack of 11.
